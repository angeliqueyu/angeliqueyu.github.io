About Me

As a world traveller, I was a cabin crew working for Singapore Airlines, a junior assistant cruise director for Princess Cruises cruising around British Isles, a passenger on huge cargo ship crossing the Atlantic. When younger, I was an exchange student in Montreal, a working-holiday tourist on farms in both Canada and Japan. I love to explore different cultures and meet people from different backgrounds, which let me understand how big the world is and how small I am. Being Humble and respecting each other are always the attitude I expect myself to have towards this world.

I had majored in Business Administration in university before. In these years of exploring the world and discovery of myself after graduation, with abundant international experience, finally I found the desire and motivation to be back to this major. I am professional in people management, communications and crisis management. In addition, I am able to work efficiently on a team or alone.
